Patents

Title :

Title : Advanced teeth cleaning device (Tooth brush) with paste filling bucket.

ABSTRACT :

Our invention “Advanced cleaning device (tooth brush) with paste filling bucket. clean teeth and gums better than a manual tooth brush. It removes plaque and keeping our teeth and gum as healthy as possible. Therefore, we have proposed a new advanced teeth cleaning device (tooth brush) Which is having six different features will help to maintain oral health. cleaning device have paste filling bucket will help to avoid the daily put the paste on brush manually and avoid the extra waste of paste in tube. The proposed structure of tooth brush has three-part head part, middle part and bottom paste filling bucket. It has been provided with button to switch on and off. The speed of the cleaning action can also be controlled using button. The device is capable of generating a report about visiting dentist, the possible decay which can cause serious pain, the possible Teeth decay .it can also be scheduled to alter the use of the brushing them. Toothpaste is also stored in the device and as per the requirement it is oozed out of the pores when the user switches on the device. simply pushing on the slider with your hands will effortlessly move the toothpaste from the bucket to the brush head.
